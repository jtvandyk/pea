## 🎯 THE KEY INNOVATION: PREDICTION-POWERED INFERENCE

**The Problem**: LLMs misclassify events, so your statistics are biased

**The Solution**: Use Prediction-Powered Inference (Angelopoulos et al. 2023)

**Example**:
```
❌ WRONG: "45% are demonstrations"
✅ RIGHT: "42% [38%-47% 95% CI]"
```

**Why it matters**: Second answer accounts for LLM classification errors
